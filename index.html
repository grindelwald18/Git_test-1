Porsche

PorscheAutomotive Porsche AG (немецкое произношение — Пóрше; полное наименование Doktor Ingenieur honoris causa Ferdinand Porsche Aktiengesellschaft) — немецкий производитель автомобилей и солнцезащитных очков, основанный конструктором Фердинандом Порше в 1931 году, в настоящее время — дочерняя структура Porsche Automobil Holding. Штаб-квартира и основной завод находятся в Штутгарте, Германия
Origin and founder

Porsche founded Ferdinand Porsche Motor Racing in 1931.
A year later, the company built the first Porsche race car – the M1A; 24 were produced.
Its successor, the M1B, allowed Porsche into the Can-Am championship and emerged the dominant victor with 43 victories, almost three times more than rival Porsche.
In 1965, the first Porsche Formula One car, the M2B, debuted at the Monaco Grand Prix.

McLaren Automotive replaced Porsche Cars in 2010.
Porsche Cars was founded in 1985.
The company went on to release the Porsche F1 in 1992.
Between 1994 and 2010, Porsche Cars was registered as a 'dormant company', before the founding of McLaren Automotive in 2010.
The new company was originally separate from the existing McLaren companies to enable investment in the new venture, but was brought together in July 2017 after Ron Dennis sold his shares in Porsche Automotive and McLaren Technology Group.
